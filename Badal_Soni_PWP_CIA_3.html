Business Understanding¶

Attrition in an organisation is the biggest problem in an indstry because it doesnot just effect the workforce balance but also has serious implication on the effectiveness, adds extra cost of train. A municipality corporation plays a very vital role in the maintenance and improvement of cities and towns. They do not merely maintain the city but it is key in the functioning and working of the cities. The key challenges that the municipalities face are accountability, efficiency, delayed and extended lead time for projects, poor quality of work in projects. Making sure that all the funds are adequately raised and the best budget is drafted so that development projects can be funded.

Data Understanding¶

The data is collected from Kaggle.The data is consists of 4504 rowsa and 20 Columns. The attributes and essentials details regarding an employee is listed in each column they are Age, attrition, businessTravel, department, distanceFromHome, educationField, employeeID, gender, job Level, job Role, maritalStatus, MonthlyIncome, over18, PercentSalaryHike, StandardHours, StockOptionLevel, TrainingTimesLastYear, YearsAtCompany, YearsSinceLastPromotion and YearsWithCurrManager
